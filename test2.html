Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership across multiple sectors

Founded with a vision to transform industries and create lasting value, OSKA has grown from a small startup to a diversified company operating across oil & gas, automotive, agriculture, real estate, and strategic acquisitions.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that excellence in service delivery, combined with innovative solutions and unwavering integrity, could make a meaningful difference in the lives of our clients and communities.

Today, we continue to build on that foundation, expanding our reach while maintaining the personal touch and commitment to quality that has defined us from the beginning.
